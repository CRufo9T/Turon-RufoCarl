Turon

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of saba (banana)
  • 1 cup of jackfruit
  • 1Â½ cups sugar
  • 12 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 2 cups of cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Step 1. Peel the saba bananas and cut them in half lengthwise. Slice the jackfruit into thin strips.
  2. Step 2. Place a spring roll wrapper on a clean, flat surface. Lay a banana slice diagonally across the wrapper. Add a strip or two of jackfruit on top of the banana. Sprinkle about 1 tablespoon of brown sugar over the fruit. Fold the bottom corner of the wrapper over the filling, then fold in the sides. Roll the wrapper tightly, sealing the edge with a bit of water to keep it closed.
  3. Step 3. Heat a generous amount of cooking oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully place the wrapped turon into the pan. Sprinkle a little brown sugar directly into the oil (this will caramelize and coat the turon). Fry the turon until golden brown and the sugar has melted into a glaze, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  4. Step 4. Remove the fried turon and place it on a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil. Let them cool slightly before serving.
